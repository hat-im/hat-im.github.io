How to solve a cryptogram
In a cryptogram, every time a letter appears in the coded message, it corresponds to the same decoded letter. In order to solve a cryptogram, you should start by solving the short, common words, such as "in" or "of". Replacing the most common letters may also be a helpful way to start. The most common letters in English are E, T, A, O, and N, with S and I close behind. As you discern some of the letters, you should attempt to figure out the longer words.
